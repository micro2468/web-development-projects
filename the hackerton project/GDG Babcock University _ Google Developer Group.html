About GDG Babcock

We are a community of passionate developers and tech enthusiasts at Babcock University

Google Developer Groups (GDG) are community groups for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology.

At GDG Babcock, we organize workshops, hackathons, speaker sessions and more to help students grow their technical skills and network with industry professionals.

  • Free workshops & training
  • Networking opportunities
  • Hands-on coding sessions
  • Access to Google resources
